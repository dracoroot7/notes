# 🇪🇸 Spanish Beginner Mastery Plan (3 Months)
**Goal:** Reach A2 (Beginner-Intermediate) conversational level.  
**Commitment:** 1 hour per day.

---

## 🛠 I. Essential Resources

### 📺 Video & Audio (Input)
* **[Dreaming Spanish](https://www.youtube.com/@DreamingSpanish):** The best source for "Comprehensible Input." Start with the "Superbeginner" playlist.
* **[Butterfly Spanish](https://www.youtube.com/@ButterflySpanish):** Excellent for clear explanations of grammar, verbs, and pronunciation.
* **[Destinos](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLmgeO5v_V_pE6T6L7n_HAn8uF3TfXvTvt):** A structured "educational telenovela" designed specifically for beginners.
* **[Extr@ Spanish](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL-w7A9pKNKxZ9C9WvG_W_pZidj6mDof34):** A sitcom-style show (like *Friends*) with simplified Spanish vocabulary.

### 🗂 Vocabulary & Apps
* **[Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net/):** The gold standard for flashcards. Search for a "Spanish 1000 Common Words" deck.
* **[Memrise](https://www.memrise.com/):** A more visual, gamified alternative for learning essential vocabulary.

---

## 🤖 II. Gemini 1-on-1 Setup & Practice
Gemini Live (Voice Mode) allows you to practice speaking without the "stage fright" of a human tutor.

### **How to Start a Session:**
1. Open the **Gemini App** on your phone.
2. Tap the **Live (waveform icon)** at the bottom right to enter voice mode.
3. Paste (or say) the **Tutor Prompt** below to set the ground rules.

### **The "Tutor Prompt" (Copy/Paste this):**
> "Act as a patient Spanish language tutor. I am a total beginner. I want to practice a conversation about **[Insert Topic, e.g., 'ordering food' or 'my hobbies']**. 
> 1. Please speak in simple Spanish, then provide the English translation immediately after.
> 2. If I make a mistake, stop and gently correct me.
> 3. Ask me one short question at a time to keep the conversation going.
> 4. Let's start! Greet me in Spanish."

## 🗓 III. The 90-Day Roadmap

### **Month 1: The Foundation**
* **15m Flashcards:** Learn 10 new words daily.
* **30m Input:** Watch [Dreaming Spanish Superbeginner](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLlpPf-YgbU7GrtxP9ohIVEGWbwbiL70D8).
* **15m Gemini Live:** Practice "The Basics" (Greetings, alphabet, and "About Me").

### **Month 2: Building Sentences**
* **10m Flashcards:** Focus on high-frequency verbs (to want, to go, to have).
* **30m TV Series:** Watch one episode of [Destinos](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLmgeO5v_V_pE6T6L7n_HAn8uF3TfXvTvt).
* **20m Gemini Live:** Roleplay "Survival Scenarios" (At a restaurant, asking for directions, shopping).

### **Month 3: Conversational Flow**
* **10m Flashcards:** Vocabulary maintenance.
* **25m Input:** Advance to [Dreaming Spanish Beginner](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLlpPf-YgbU7H86vG6pXW_f-6D1mZ9vOa1).
* **25m Gemini Live:** Storytelling practice. Tell Gemini about your weekend or a movie you like using the past tense.

## ✅ Pro-Tips for Success
1. **Don't Use English Subtitles:** If you can't understand a video with Spanish subs, it is too difficult. Go back to easier content.
2. **Shadowing:** When watching YouTube, pause and repeat sentences out loud to train your tongue muscles.
3. **Consistency:** 1 hour a day is significantly more effective than a 7-hour "cram session" on Sundays.
